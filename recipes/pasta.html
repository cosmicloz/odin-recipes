Vegetable Pasta Sauce

Description

This pasta sauce is simple yet delicious. I like to serve it over chickpea pasta for added protein.

Ingredients

  • 1 small can of tomato paste
  • Bell peppers
  • Onion
  • Garlic cloves (2-4 depending on how garlicky you like it)
  • Olive oil
  • Grated parmesan or Italian cheese blend
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic powder

Steps

  1. Chop the onions and peppers and mince the garlic cloves.
  2. Heat a bit of olive oil in a saucepan and saute the peppers, onions, and garlic until the onion starts to brown.
  3. Add the tomato paste and stir it all together, adding more olive oil if the consistency is too thick.
  4. Add salt, pepper, and garlic powder to taste.
  5. Mix in grated parmesan or Italian blend, allowing it to melt throughout the sauce.
  6. Serve over pasta.
