Simple Sponge Cake

Description

This sponge cake was one of the first recipes I ever learned how to make. It is super easy and has many variations.

Ingredients

  • Two large eggs
  • Softened butter
  • Sugar
  • Flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • Powdered sugar (for making the frosting)
  • Optional: Lemon juice and grated lemon rind if you want to make it a lemon cake.

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Weigh the two eggs and measure out the same weight of butter, flour, and sugar.
  3. Combine the butter and sugar in a mixing bowl until the mixture looks fluffy.
  4. Beat in the two eggs.
  5. Beat in the flour.
  6. Add the baking powder.
  7. Add the lemon juice and rind if you want to make it into a lemon cake.
  8. Grease and flour a cake tin and pour the batter into the tin.
  9. Bake the cake in the preheated oven for about 20 minutes, or until the top is golden and springy to the touch.
  10. Combine powdered sugar and water (or lemon juice) to make a glaze for the top of the cake.
  11. When the cake is cool, pour the glaze over the top.
  12. Enjoy!
