Hearty Scrambled Eggs

Description

This sponge cake was one of the first recipes I ever learned how to make. It is super easy and has many variations.

Ingredients

  • Two or three eggs
  • Butter
  • Bell peppers
  • Onion
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Melt a dollop of butter in a frying pan.
  2. Chop up the peppers and onion and saute until tender.
  3. Add the eggs and cook until only a little runny.
  4. Grate in the cheese and mix it all together to melt it evenly throughout the eggs.
  5. Add salt and pepper to taste and serve with buttered toast.
